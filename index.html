About Me

I was born in Sicily, Italy, one of the most beautiful islands in the Mediterranean. When I lived there I really enjoyed the sun, the sea and the beautiful nature this land has to offer.
I have lived in England since 2012. I have experienced the exciting scene of London for 6 years but my wife and I decided to move to Manchester a couple of years ago.
I have always showed passion for languages, music and IT. My love for coding began at school when I studied older coding languages such as Assembler and Pascal. After completing my A levels, I learnt other programming languages and I worked on different projects.
In the past 2 years I have sharpened my abilities, I have deepened my knowledge and strengthened my skills but I am still hungry to learn! At the moment I am working as a freelancer and each time I work on a website I manage the whole project. I don't do the coding only: I get to know the client and ascertain their goals, I design logos and icons, I adapt and edit pictures.
In my free time I love watching and playing football and my guitar is always next to my desk, ready to accompany me when belting out during a break. My wife and I love exploring the world! As Italians, we are passionate about food and when we travel we enjoy meeting the locals, share a delicious meal and learn about their culture.
